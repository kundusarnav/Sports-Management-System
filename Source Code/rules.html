Rules for Participating in Sports



Following rules must be known for participating on-line:

(1) Participant must be the citizen of Vadodara.

(2) Participant have to fill entire details to be a part of team.

(3) Participant must have proper number of players in particular sports:
     (a) For cricket, there must be atleast 11 players.
     (b) For football, there must be atleast 11 players.
     (c) For racing, individual will be only considered.
     (d) For handball, individual will be only considered.

(4) Money will be payed online after completing the task of participation.

(5) Rs. 500 will be collected on-line for the whole transaction.
